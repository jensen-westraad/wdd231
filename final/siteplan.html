Pokemon TCG Visual Lists Siteplan

Site Name: "Leafeon's Leaflet"

Reason: The Pokemon Mascot for the Website is my favorite pokemon, "Leafeon" which is a green, grassy themed pokemon that inspired the websites color scheme and overall peaceful feel.

Site Purpose:

The site provides a full list of each card in a specific card set of the Pokemon TCG. The reason this has value is that it provides a easy visual hub for beginners to decide what cards they would like to chase or for experts to know all the cards needed to master the set. The site also gives an opportunity to join a membership program that provides monthly updates on the market, new set coming out and free monthly packs of there favourite set for being a member.

Scenarios:

What cards are found in this set?

Is the card I want found in this set?

What cards do I need to Master this set?

What are specifics about one card in the set?

Why should I try this set?
